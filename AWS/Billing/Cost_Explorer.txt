====== Cost Explorer ======
Created Sunday 07 April 2019

==== Overview ====
The cost explorer are your financial logs for AWS. Itnot only allow you to view past spending habits, it also forcasts future spending as well.

Detailed Breakdown of AWS Costs can be performed by the Cost Explorer. Reports can be saved and deleted.

To get to cost explorer
https://console.aws.amazon.com/cost-reports/home#/dashboard

1) Billing Dash Board
2) Cost Explorer
3) Select one of the preconfigured views, or select a blank view. You can filter on many things.
