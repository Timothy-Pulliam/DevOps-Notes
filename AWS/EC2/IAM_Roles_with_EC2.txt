====== IAM Roles with EC2 ======
Created Monday 30 July 2018

https://docs.aws.amazon.com/IAM/latest/UserGuide/id_roles_create_for-service.html?icmpid=docs_iam_console
Roles are the preffered way to grant user's permissions to access AWS resources. They are easier to provision, administer and revoke than Access Key IDs and Secret Access Keys.
