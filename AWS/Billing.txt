====== Billing ======
Created Friday 06 July 2018

==== The Billing Dashboard ====
To get to the billing dashboard
https://console.aws.amazon.com/billing/home

{{.\pasted_image003.png}}

1) We see the monthly cost projection for AWS services. The blue shows costs so far. The green shows estimated costs for the end of the billing period.
2) We see what AWS services cost the most

{{.\pasted_image.png}}


The bills tab allows us to drill down further and see **why** we are being charged. AWS always charges the minimum amount ($0.01) for services being used. You can also filter by region.

{{.\pasted_image001.png}}


==== Billing Calculator ====
You can estimate how much AWS will cost by using the Billing Calculator
https://calculator.s3.amazonaws.com/index.html

You can even use the price calculator to calculate common scenarios and their pricing
