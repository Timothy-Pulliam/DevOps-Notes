====== Best Practices ======
Created Saturday 18 August 2018

==== File Naming ====
https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Learn/Getting_started_with_the_web/Dealing_with_files#An_aside_on_casing_and_spacing

Do:
* lower case
* comma separated
* my-file.html
* test-site/myimage.jpg

Don't:
* test-site/MyImage.jpg
* my_file.html
